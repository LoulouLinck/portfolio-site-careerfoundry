About me

With a background in History, I turned my skills in treating qualitative data towards quantitative data by seeking for further studies in backend development. A cultural mediator, I worked with an international team of 50 colleagues and a wide spectrum of publics collecting their feedback through debates and continuous reporting. This allowed the visitor experience to improve considerably and to adapt holistically to visitors’ concerns. Having worked extensively with various audiences, I am keen on presenting data to users with their best interest in mind. So I went full-stack! With a focus on people-centred cultural management as a scholar, I now am excited to contribute to philantropic products at a crossroad between Culture and Tech industries.
